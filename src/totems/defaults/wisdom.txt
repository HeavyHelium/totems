Five faults and eight antidotes: a shamatha outline where five meditation obstacles are met by eight cultivated remedies.
Five faults: 1. laziness; 2. forgetting the instruction; 3. agitation and dullness; 4. non-application; 5. over-application.
Eight antidotes: 1. faith; 2. aspiration; 3. effort; 4. pliancy; 5. mindfulness; 6. awareness; 7. application; 8. equanimity.
Mapping: laziness -> faith, aspiration, effort, pliancy; forgetting -> mindfulness; agitation/dullness -> awareness; non-application -> application; over-application -> equanimity.
